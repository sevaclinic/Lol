Return & Exchange Policy

We offer refunds and exchanges within the first 7 days from the date of your purchase. If 7 days have passed since your purchase, you will not be offered a return, exchange, or refund of any kind.

Eligibility for Return or Exchange

To become eligible for a return or an exchange:

  • The purchased item should be unused and in the same condition as received.
  • The item must have its original packaging.
  • Items bought under sale may not be eligible for return or exchange.
  • Only items found to be defective or damaged will be replaced after inspection.

Inspection and Processing

Once we receive your returned product, our team will inspect it and send you an email notification. If the returned item is approved, your return or exchange will be processed within 2 working days. The exchanged product will be delivered within 7 working days.

How to Initiate a Return/Exchange

To initiate a return or exchange, contact our customer support team with:

  • Order number
  • Details of the item(s) you wish to return/exchange
  • Reason for return
  • Photos of the item if it is damaged or defective

Note: We reserve the right to refuse returns that do not meet our eligibility criteria.
