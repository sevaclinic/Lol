About us

Get to know the story behind Seva Clinic Clothing.

Seva Clinic Clothing is a small, home–grown ethnic boutique based in Udaynarayanpur, Howrah.

We focus on simple, wearable Indian outfits that feel good in daily life – cotton kurtis, sarees, lehenga sets and more. Our goal is to save your time by showing clear photos, honest descriptions and straightforward pricing.

Seva Clinic was established in 2020 and has been serving customers across India since 2021. When you shop with us, you are supporting a small business that cares about every order.
